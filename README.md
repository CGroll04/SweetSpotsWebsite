# SweetSpots Website

Official landing page for SweetSpots.

## About

SweetSpots helps users save, organize, and share restaurants, cafés, attractions, and other places they discover through social media, friends, and everyday life.

Instead of losing recommendations in screenshots, bookmarks, notes, or direct messages, SweetSpots keeps everything in one place and makes it easy to revisit, organize, and share.

## Features

- Save places discovered on social media
- Organize spots into custom collections
- Share collections and recommendations with friends
- Receive proximity notifications for saved locations
- Access maps and navigation for saved spots
- Discover and revisit places you've saved over time
